Butterfly

A butterfly is an insect known for its beautiful, colorful wings and distinctive life cycle. It belongs to the order Lepidoptera, which also includes moths. Butterflies go through four life stages: egg, larva (caterpillar), pupa (chrysalis), and adult. This process is called metamorphosis.
